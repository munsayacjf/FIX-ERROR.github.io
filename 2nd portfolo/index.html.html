What I do

Idea + Sketching

The Sketch phase is the longest part of the process, and it's because it's when we'll get to bond! This is where honesty plays a huge role, and the back and forth is an essential part of ensuring that the sketch comes out as the perfect logo.

Illustrating

The Digital phase is the quickest of them all. This is when I take the sketch that was agreed upon, and I run it through Adobe Illustrator. Here, I clean the logo up, apply colors, measure spacing, and try out different lockups that can work for different applications. When it's all shiny and neat, I present it back to you for the two thumbs up.

Graphics Design

Here I create my poster and banner designs. I usually make a mood board for the theme of the design before I go to the editing process. When it's not for a client, it usually takes time to decide what kind of design I'm about to do.
